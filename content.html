SUNDAYS

The church is more than just a place to attend on a Sunday morning.

Our church family gathers together in ways similar to a natural family. Every Sunday morning, we gather in our church services to worship, learn from the word of God, pray, and encourage one another in our respective journeys. Many who cannot gather in person, find meaningful community online sharing life, and pursuing Jesus together.

GROUPS

Small groups are an opportunity to pursue Jesus while loving one another and one of the best ways to get plugged into our community. No group is perfect but we know there’s a group that’s right for you, whether it tackles the topic of marriage, family, women, recovery, outreach and more.

MANNAHOUSE KIDS

Mannahouse Kids is committed to leading the next generation into the presence of God all while having fun doing it! We strive to support parents as they raise their children to love Jesus, love the church, and love His Word. The Dream Team is prepared to care for your little ones in a safe and secure environment, share God’s love with them, and inspire them to live big lives for Jesus every day.

MANNAHOUSE YOUTH

Once a week, Mannahouse Youth gets together to hang out with God, pray, and connect with new friends. For middle schoolers and high schoolers, we meet every Wednesday evening to be filled and inspired by each other and God’s word. Not to mention the fun camps that are held, internships, and youth conferences that have youth all over the world worshipping in one place.

MANNAHOUSE YOUNG ADULTS

Our Young Adults groups are for 18-30-somethings and a place to connect with each other, get connected to the Mannahouse family, encounter Jesus and grow in relationship with God. Our gatherings are held across five campuses on varying days and times. Our community is found in the groups that connect us.

Visit our groups page to join a young adult group
